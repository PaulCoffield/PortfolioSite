Like X, For Y

Very much a work in progress.

Having read a lot of articles about UX/UI portfolios, it became clear that a number of newer designers were seeking out ideas for projects to add. I know that it can be difficult to build a portfolio when you haven't got a one to get you projects to work on in the first place.

A few scribbles in my notepad, a quick refresher on some simple JavaScript and I had the beginnings of what I'd hoped for. A fairly quick and easy way to generate ideas for UX or UI projects.

Everything is hard-coded and the output is only visible in the console, but this project shall soon be available to all who wish to find the beginning of an idea - what they choose to do with it will be up to them, but I look forward to seeing the results that others come up with.
